About

The most important issues deserve the best storytellers.

The Re3 Hackathon is an experiment, but damn, it’s an important one. It’s time to expand the boundaries of justice-focused persuasive storytelling!

We’re hand-picking non-profits and changemakers who have stories that the world needs to hear. Maybe it’s a new take on climate change, or restorative justice, or a clever campaign on upward mobility.

We’re offering storytellers – creative strategists, design geeks, data scientists, developers, writers, filmmakers, cartoonists, and more – the chance to focus on one issue and work with top-notch teammates. Over the Re3 Hackathon weekend, we will innovate new ways of thinking and communicating stories in language, written, visualized, performed and coded.

An interactive exhibit? Excellent. Flash mob? Sure. App? Why not. Sculpture? Perfect. Print piece? Absolutely. There is absolutely no limitation of medium for the teams’ final outputs as long as they tell captivating, powerful stories.

Creatives with a conscience, start your engines.

The Issues

We believe that everyone deserves a fair chance. Let’s amplify the voices seeking to make the world a fairer, more just place, even if it means challenging the powers that be!

Why Storytelling

The Re3 Hackathon is an experiment in pushing the boundaries of justice-based persuasive storytelling.

Let’s be honest. We all have issues that we care about, and that we’d like others to agree with. While it’s easy to march out an army of facts and hope that they overwhelm people into thinking differently, that usually isn’t enough to change hearts and minds.

  • 1
    We wont preach to the choir.

    Let’s articulate why these stories matter to those that think they don’t.

  • 2
    We’ll start with empathy.

    Let’s listen and try to understand different perspectives.

  • 3
    We’ll find the human side to the facts.

    Let’s be rigorous, but always put people before numbers.

  • 4
    We’ll tell beautiful stories.

    Stories that people different than us can connect to, relate to, and be open to.

  • 5
    We’ll pull out all the stops.

    We’ll use humor, love, metaphor, sex, and aything under the sun so our stories are heard.
